## Executive Summary

**FeedbackPro** is a SaaS platform for creative teams to review, annotate, and approve video, image, and document files. We solve the problem of scattered feedback across email, Slack, and video calls.

**Target Market**: Video production studios, marketing agencies, design teams, e-learning companies
**Business Model**: Freemium SaaS with tiered pricing
**Revenue Goal Year 1**: $100,000 MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)

## üí∞ Revenue Model

### Pricing Tiers

#### Free Forever
- **Price**: $0
- **Storage**: 5GB
- **Projects**: 2 active projects
- **Users**: Unlimited collaborators
- **Features**: Basic commenting, timestamping
- **Goal**: Acquisition & viral growth

#### Pro
- **Price**: $29/month (billed monthly) or $24/month (billed annually)
- **Storage**: 100GB
- **Projects**: Unlimited
- **Features**: 
  - Advanced annotations
  - Version history (10 versions)
  - Priority email support
  - Custom branding (watermark removal)
- **Target**: Freelancers, small teams (1-5 people)

#### Team
- **Price**: $99/month (billed monthly) or $79/month (billed annually)
- **Storage**: 500GB
- **Users**: Up to 20 team members
- **Features**:
  - Everything in Pro
  - Team management & roles
  - Advanced permissions
  - API access
  - Integrations (Slack, Asana, Jira)
  - Video call button
  - Analytics dashboard
- **Target**: Growing agencies, production companies

#### Enterprise
- **Price**: Custom (starting at $499/month)
- **Storage**: Unlimited or custom
- **Features**:
  - Everything in Team
  - White-label option
  - On-premise deployment option
  - SSO (SAML)
  - Dedicated account manager
  - 99.9% SLA guarantee
  - Custom integrations
  - Training & onboarding
- **Target**: Large agencies, broadcasters, Fortune 500

### Revenue Projections

**Year 1 Goals:**
- Month 3: 100 free users, 10 paying ($290 MRR)
- Month 6: 500 free users, 50 paying ($1,450 MRR)
- Month 9: 2,000 free users, 150 paying ($4,350 MRR)
- Month 12: 5,000 free users, 300 paying ($8,700 MRR)

**Conversion Rates:**
- Free ‚Üí Pro: 5%
- Pro ‚Üí Team: 15%

**Churn Target:** < 5% monthly

## üõ°Ô∏è Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risks

1. **Competition from Frame.io (Adobe)**
   - **Mitigation**: Focus on price & simplicity for SMBs
   - **Advantage**: Adobe's enterprise focus leaves SMB gap

2. **Storage Costs Scale**
   - **Mitigation**: Start with generous but capped free tier
   - **Solution**: Implement auto-archiving, compression

3. **Slow User Growth**
   - **Mitigation**: Aggressive content marketing + Product Hunt
   - **Backup**: Pivot to agency white-label model

4. **Technical Challenges (Video)**
   - **Mitigation**: Start with simple features, add advanced later
   - **Solution**: Use proven libraries (FFmpeg, HLS)

5. **Churn Due to Feature Gaps**
   - **Mitigation**: Fast iteration based on user feedback
   - **Strategy**: Monthly feature releases
